Research Profile

Spatial analysis for mountain cryosphere systems

My work combines geospatial modelling, field-based validation, and remote-sensing datasets to study rock glacier geometry, kinematics, and permafrost-related change. I focus on producing reproducible analyses and publication-quality figures that make complex spatial patterns easier to interpret.
