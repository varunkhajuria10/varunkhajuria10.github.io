Research Profile

Spatial analysis for mountain cryosphere systems

I am a PhD Researcher at the University of Pisa working on rock glaciers and alpine permafrost. My research focuses on estimating ice thickness, numerical modelling, and the use of geophysical tools to investigate mountain cryosphere systems. I use GIS, Python, PCRaster, and remote sensing to analyse spatial processes, validate model outputs, and communicate scientific results.

Selected projects and research outputs

Numerical modelling | Mountain cryosphere

Rock Glacier Ice-Thickness Modelling

This project estimates the internal ice thickness of rock glaciers using numerical modelling and spatial datasets. The workflow integrates terrain-derived variables, rock glacier geometry, and physically informed assumptions to produce distributed ice-thickness outputs for alpine permafrost landforms.

  • Research focus: rock glaciers, alpine permafrost, subsurface ice estimation.
  • Methods: numerical modelling, raster analysis, terrain analysis, geospatial processing.
  • Tools: Python, PCRaster, QGIS, GIS workflows, scientific visualization.
  • Outputs: ice-thickness maps, model rasters, summary figures, and interpretation of spatial patterns.

Published image album | April 2022 | IIRS, ISRO

Himalayan Glaciers: As Viewed from Indian Earth Observation Satellites

Co-authored image album published by the Indian Institute of Remote Sensing, Dehradun, India. The album presents Himalayan glaciers through Indian Earth Observation satellite datasets and remote-sensing visual interpretation.

Google Earth Engine | Machine learning | Satellite embeddings

Rock Glacier Detection Using Satellite Embeddings and Machine Learning

Open-source Google Earth Engine workflow for mapping rock glaciers using AlphaEarth satellite embeddings and supervised machine learning. The script supports classifier comparison, accuracy assessment, area estimation, and export of classified rock glacier rasters.

  • Repository: GEE_RG_Detection.
  • Methods: KNN, Random Forest, Support Vector Machine, and Gradient Tree Boost classifiers.
  • Data: AlphaEarth satellite embeddings and Sentinel-2 visual reference composites.
  • Output: classified rasters, accuracy charts, area estimates, RGB composites, and training summaries.
  • Citation: Khajuria, V., Singh, S., Paro, L. et al. Rock Glacier Detection Using Satellite Embeddings and Machine Learning on Google Earth Engine. Earth Systems and Environment (2026).
